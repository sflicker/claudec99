Stages 94 and 95-01 through 95-11 added no language features; they harden the self-host cycle (stage 94's `--mode=self-host`) and complete an internal **dynamic-storage refactor** — new `Vec` and `StrBuf` containers replace every fixed-capacity parser/codegen table (locals, globals, string pool, enum constants/tags, struct/union tags, typedefs, functions, break/switch stacks, user labels, struct-field scratch), and all token, AST, parser, and codegen name/tag/label text moves from `char[MAX_NAME_LEN]` buffers into `const char *` pointers backed by a lexer-owned string pool, removing the old per-table caps and the 255-byte string-literal limit (and fixing three latent silent-overflow bugs along the way).
